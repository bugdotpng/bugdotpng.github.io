1.04: Boxes //////

Rami placed a tape recorder between them. "Now, this interview has a couple different sections. First, I'm gonna ask you five biographical questions, then five objective questions, then five open-ended questions. Answer normally, you can be as casual as you like. Just be honest."

Oral Interview Transcript: Daniel Suárez, Secondary Adjunct

Q1

At the time of this interview, are you infected with a Vice?

A1

Yes.

Q2

What is the classification?

A2

It's complicated.

Rami clicked off the recording. "Listen, I know it's complicated, but I don't want this interview getting flagged. For the sake of our sanity, both of ours, just play along."

"Play along how?"

"Just say what's in your file."

Q2

What is the classification?

A2

It's complicated. Addendum 1 I received a Phi Vice classification from CVRS when I was younger.

Q3

Are you currently seeing a Vice Management Specialist for treatment through CVRS?

A3

Not officially, no.

Q4

Had you experienced a Branch prior to B1473610100-23?

A4

What the hell is that?

Rami clicked off the recording again and dragged his hands down his face with an exasperated sigh. "Oh my god, Danny we're not even at the hard questions yet."

"You can't just start throwing numbers at me and act like I have any idea what you're talking about."

"Context clues! That's the system code for Emory's Branch! The one you were just in!"

"Why didn’t you just say that?"

"Protocol, dammit! Just answer the question!"

Q4

Had you experienced a Branch prior to B1473610100-23?

A4

What the hell is that? Addendum 1 What was the question?

Rami smothered the recorder with his hands and leaned toward Danny with grit teeth, "Do you want to get out of here today? I'll keep you locked up here for as long as I need to. Don't test me, Suárez."

Q4

Had you experienced a Branch prior to B1473610100-23?

A4

What the hell is that? Addendum 1 What was the question? Addendum 2 Technically, yes.

Q5

Had you been an Adjunct prior to B1473610100-23?

A5

No. Are you going to have to say that every time?

Agent Interjection B1473610100-23 will be referred to simply as Emory’s Branch from this point in the interview moving forward.

Adjunct Interjection Oh, thank god.

Q6

What triggered Emory's Branch?

A6

Oh, I, uh...I dunno. I was kinda preoccupied with my own crisis before all this happened. I guess they were a little frazzled after Frankie talked with them. So it was probably that.

Q6

What triggered Emory's Branch? Clarification Addendum 1 Was there a single inciting incident that led to the Branch's initiation?

A6

Oh, I, uh...I dunno. I was kinda preoccupied with my own crisis before all this happened. I guess they were a little frazzled after Frankie talked with them. So it was probably that. Addendum 1 Like I said, the whole Frankie thing, probably. I really don’t know. I’m not gonna pretend like I know what’s going on in their head half the time.

Q6

What triggered Emory's Branch? Clarification Addendum 1 Was there a single inciting incident that led to the Branch's initiation? Clarification Addendum 2 What was happening when the Branch was initiated?

A6

Oh, I, uh...I dunno. I was kinda preoccupied with my own crisis before all this happened. I guess they were a little frazzled after Frankie talked with them. So it was probably that. Addendum 1 Like I said, the whole Frankie thing, probably. I really don’t know. I’m not gonna pretend like I know what’s going on in their head half the time. Addendum 2 Are you implying something?

End 1.04: Boxes

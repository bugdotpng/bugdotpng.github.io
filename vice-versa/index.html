Vice Versa //////

Danny is a depressed convenience store cashier who truly just wants to live a normal life. But in a world infested with the parasitic creatures known as Vices, this is far from a simple thing to achieve—especially when he has one of his own. With the help of his good friends Gen and Rami, he has learned how to lead a relatively uneventful life, but the growing feeling of unease has been getting harder to ignore. And when the raging spitfire Emory becomes a more sustained force in his life, he can feel himself on the edge of a breakdown.

Vice Versa

More than anything, Danny just wants to live a normal life, but in a world infested with the parasitic creatures known as Vices, this is far from a simple thing to achieve—especially when he has one of his own. With the help of his good friends Gen and Rami, he has learned how to keep things under control, but the growing uneasiness has been getting harder to ignore. And when the raging spitfire Emory becomes a more sustained force in his life, he can feel himself on the verge of a breakdown.

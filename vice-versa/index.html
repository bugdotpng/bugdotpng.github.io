Vice Versa

Danny simply yearns for a peaceful, uneventful life, but in a world infested with the parasitic creatures known as Vices, this is far from a simple thing to achieve—especially when he’s infected with one of his own. After decades of learning to navigate the complexities of Vices, he has convinced himself he can do it alone. But when the unpredictable spitfire Emory becomes a more sustained presence in his life, he can feel things slowly slipping out of his control.

Content Warning

Themes of medical neglect are present throughout this story, in addition to recurring medical imagery. If you are sensitive to medical topics, this may not be the story for you!

This story also includes references to disordered eating, suicide, assisted suicide, parental abuse, self harm, excessive vomiting, parasitism, gore, body horror, and decapitation.

Relevant content warnings will also be provided at the top of each chapter.

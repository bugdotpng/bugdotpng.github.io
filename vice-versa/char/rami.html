Advent

The patient experienced a sudden advent at age 15, initiated due what the patient described as a panic attack, which was triggered after witnessing a public dispersal event (B2081320503-08). The patient claims to have passed the Host as they initiated the process of dispersal and was directly within the dispersal radius. Emotions present at advent included helplessness, despair, and fear. Smells included cinnamon, popcorn and floral perfume, and tastes included a soft pretzel and blue raspberry slush. The sudden advent resulted in B1472940300-01, wherein the patient was able to remove himself from the situation during staging to seclude himself during production. B1472940300-01 was experienced alone. The exhibition was relatively tame with the stocky build of a large vermin, much like a cross between an armadillo and possum. The Branch Arena was simple, mimicking the emergency exit hallway he had secluded himself in. Patient was able to successfully commit B1472940300-01 after damaging the core using manual force.

Patch Notes

Patient’s patch was summoned between B1472940300-03 and B1472940300-04, shortly after the core shed for the first time. Patient notified his Vice Management Specialist but performed patch establishment personally, aided by his partner Genesis Schwarzschild. The design of the patch likely pulls influence from the patient’s repeated visits to a local axe-throwing club, of which he frequented as an after-school activity for stress management.

Efficacy

Despite the exhibition’s stout build, it is quite speedy and requires precision to effectively damage, as it prioritizes dodging over attacking to prolong Branch times. Having a dual, thrown patch allows for repeated attacks from varying vantage points. When successful, the blades of these handaxes inflict considerable damage and immediately terminate the Branch. The patient’s patch is well-suited to combat his Vice, and will aid well in achieving rescission even if alone.

Branch Formula

Because of the Vice’s classification, Branch Formula changed often in the Vice’s life cycle due to shedding. After the core shed for the first time, between B1472940300-03 and B1472940300-04, the Branch formula began to pull from other stressful experiences, fundamentally altering its properties. Patient has noted a uniting theme in the Branch Formula, the stress of witnessing the suffering of a loved one, noted in his most recent Shedding Assessment.

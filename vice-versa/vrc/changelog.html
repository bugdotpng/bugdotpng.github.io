Any updates made to the VRC after April 9, 2025* will be documented and outlined below in reverse-chronological order. CVRS is committed to transparently and honestly communicating the fruits of our dedicated research team. The information presented in this compendium has undergone rigorous testing so as to ensure accuracy. Lead Station Aggregators across the country contribute to the research database, which has been reformatted and presented in a layman-friendly way as the Vice Research Compendium. As new discoveries and research breakthroughs are made and verified, they will be added to the compendium and noted below.

* Author's Note
Vice Versa takes place in mid-late summer of 2019, but for the sake of transparency, the dates here are reflective of when I'm actually releasing updates in real time, not in-universe. I don't have enough braincells to retrofit stuff, and I don't want to add another hurdle to occasionally expanding the lore here. So yeah, the dates below are not lore-compliant, lol.

April 9, 2025

v5.0.0

This is the first version of the Vice Research Compendium that is publicly available and does not require a request to be fulfilled by a Vice Management Specialist. After popular demand, we have elected to publish this information publicly so as to not place it behind a paywall; though, we still recommend seeing one of our dedicated agents to receive proper care if you believe you are infected with a Vice. Many classifications can be tricky, and not all experiences will be covered in this compendium. No amount of published research will ever be able to replace the dedicated care and attention provided by our staff.

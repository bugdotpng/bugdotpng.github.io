Our Mission

CVRS is committed to the holistic support of our communities. Whether that’s through direct contact with our Public Health & Safety sector or through the pursuit of research, we are always looking out for our community.

Contact Us

For more information, call us at (214) 877-8423 or send an email to info@cvrs.com.

Our mission at CVRS is to provide timely, accessible and holistic Vice care using data-driven treatment plans backed by an extensive body of research. Our Research sector is continually investigating new ways to approach our treatment of Vices, while our Public Health & Safety sector facilitates these innovative treatments and takes care of our communities.

We believe transparency is integral to the success of our endeavors, seen throughout this website. The efficiency and quality of our service is second to none, and our drive to serve our community is what keeps us going.

Sector Advocates

Integral to agents’ success, both sectors have a designated Sector Advocate. These agents do not engage with patients or facilitate research; instead, they perform the administrative duties for the agents of their respective sector, including hiring, employee relations, and funding requests.

PHS Sector Advocate REP-01

The PHS Sector Advocate oversees the Primary Care, Urgent Care, and Public Deployment teams. Most notably, these agents work with representatives from each team to compile status and performance reports and brainstorm strategies to address any issues unique to their station. A PHS Sector Advocate also requests funding and advocates on their sector’s behalf, requesting new gear or facility enhancements as needed.

Though these agents do not interact or engage with patients face-to-face, they are still required to complete Basic training to ensure full understanding of the inner workings of CVRS and the duties expected from their agents. The PHS Sector Advocate also facilitates administrative duties, such as hiring, onboarding, employee relations, and termination.

Research Sector Advocate REP-02

The Research Sector Advocate oversees the Aggregation, Observation, and Manufacturing teams. Most notably, these agents work with representatives from each team to compile status and performance reports and brainstorm strategies to address any issues unique to their station. A Research Sector Advocate also requests funding and advocates on their sector’s behalf, pitching potential research studies after conferring with their Research Proctor and Lead Station Aggregator.

Though these agents do not conduct or facilitate research, they are still required to complete Basic training to ensure full understanding of the inner workings of CVRS and the duties expected from their agents. The Research Sector Advocate also facilitates administrative duties, such as hiring, onboarding, employee relations, and termination.

Our Team

CVRS is divided into two sectors: Public Health & Safety and Research. Each sector has unique goals, but often collaborates with the other to aggregate or share data. Public Health & Safety is primarily public-facing and provides hands-on care, while Research primarily aggregates and studies data submitted by Public Health & Safety, with limited interaction with Hosts.

Sector

Public Health & Safety

The Public Health & Safety sector is broad in its skillset, though its main focus is on on administering medical care and treatment and providing support to patients. Within Public Health & Safety, agents with the same title can belong to different teams that with different roles. These teams consist of Primary Care, Urgent Care, and Public Deployment and respond to threats of varying urgency. Primary Care facilitates long-term care, Urgent Care addresses immediate threats, and Public Deployment mitigates threats.

Patient-Facing // Team

Primary Care

Primary Care agents provide long-term treatment for patients who have been screened and admitted by a Record Administrator. These agents are equipped with the knowledge and training to help patients along their Vice journey and even perform rescission when appropriate.

Learn More
Patient-Facing // Team

Urgent Care

Agents in Urgent Care provide acute care and treat at-risk patients from both Primary Care and Public Deployment, as well as public admissions. Urgent Care is trained to commit threatening Branches and refer patients back to Primary Care to resume or begin treatment.

Learn More
Patient-Facing // Team

Public Deployment

Agents in Public Deployment are on-call 24/7, often patrolling high-traffic hot spots, led by their resident Target Hunter. These agents respond to public Branches or can be requested to monitor a potential threat. These agents also address post-Branch cleanup to ensure no anomalies have occurred.

Sector

Research

The Research Sector is comprised of analysts, engineers and researchers working to expand our understanding of Vices while designing equipment to aid in their research endeavors. Research is split into three teams: Aggregation, Observation and Manufacturing. Aggregation compiles and sorts data, Observation attends and documents patient Branches, and Manufacturing creates the technology and gear that supports our agents.

Behind-the-Scences // Team

Aggregation

The Aggregation team primarily curates, sorts, and prioritizes information submitted by the Observation team and agents from Public Health & Safety. These agents parse large amounts of data to find patterns and changes in Vice behavior and biology.

Learn More
Behind-the-Scenes // Team

Observation

Observation is a unique team of pseudo Adjuncts who accompany both the Primary and Urgent Care teams in Branches of all types, from standard to rogue to instigated. These agents do not interfere with or have an impact on active Branches and send their observations to Aggregation for review.

Learn More
Support // Team

Manufacturing

This specialized team of designers and engineers are responsible for the gear and technology that optimize agent productivity. From the iconic Parsing Goggles to the innovative Bypasses, this team is always researching ways to make the jobs of agents easier.

Learn More
Support // Role

Sector Advocate

Integral to agents’ success, both sectors have a designated Sector Advocate. These agents do not engage with patients or facilitate research; instead, they perform the administrative duties for the agents of their respective sector, including hiring, employee relations, and funding requests.

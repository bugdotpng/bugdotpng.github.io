Vice Research Compendium

VRC • Public Version

CVRS is dedicated to the public distribution and maintenance of our research database. Knowledge is power, and when you are inflicted with a Vice, every advantage puts you one step closer to conquering your Vice journey.

A printed version of this compendium is available upon request from your Vice Management Administrator.

What is the VRC?

The Vice Research Compendium (VRC) is a curated collection of the decades of information contained in the master CVRS database. Vicology is a rather intimidating field, as the terminology and technology are often regarded as inaccessible to laymen. We wanted to change that. When patients are familiar with Vice characteristics and behaviors, they are better prepared and more receptive to treatment. This VRC is a crash course in all things Vices; though it is not a stand-in for professional treatment, the information contained in compendium arms patients with the knowledge to make informed decisions when pursuing treatment or rescission. From high-level definitions to the nitty gritty of Vice biology, the VRC has you covered.

Notice

The Vice Research Compendium uses the term “Host” to refer to those infected with a Vice. While we recognize that this is regarded as insensitive to some, due to the technical nature of the compendium, the verbiage will remain unchanged to maintain transparency.

A person is so much more than their Vice; we at CVRS understand and believe that deeply. We hope that your experience with us proves that.

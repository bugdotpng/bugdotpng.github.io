Admission Guidance

Our team of dedicated agents are equipped with years of intense training to guide you through your Vice journey. We’ll be here every step of the way. Take back control and schedule a consult today!

For more information, call us at (214) 877-8423 or send an email to info@cvrs.com.

Notice

While we do our best to serve our communities in an efficient and timely manner, due to the rise in cases over the last few years, wait times are likely to be long whether you’ve called our hotline, sent an email, or arrived in-person. We appreciate your patience.

Please self-admit to your nearest Urgent Care if you are experiencing a Vice-related emergency.

In an attempt to consolidate our communication channels, all requests have been centralized and are fielded through our hotline: (214) 877-8423 our our email: info@cvrs.com.

  • General InquiriesTo find your designated station number, check your account balance, or for general admission guidance, call (214) 877-8423 or send us an email at info@cvrs.com.
  • Request for Public Deployment ServicesIf our event patrol or Branch site cleanup services are desired, call (214) 877-8423 or send us an email at info@cvrs.com.
  • Urgent Consult RequestsAll urgent matters should be handled through Urgent Care, including recently suspended Branches. Visit your local Urgent Care to initiate self-admission. To find your designated station, call (214) 877-8423 or send us an email at info@cvrs.com. Urgent matters can be addressed at any CVRS station, though once stabilized, you will be referred to your local station.
  • Non-Urgent Consult Requests - New PatientNew patients who have experienced recent Vice activity and are not in immediate danger can visit their local Primary Care for evaluation by a Record Administrator. To find your designated station, call (214) 877-8423 or send us an email at info@cvrs.com. Non-urgent matters can only be addressed by your local CVRS station.
  • Non-Urgent Consult Requests - Established PatientEstablished patients who have experienced recent Vice activity and are not in immediate danger can visit their local Primary Care for evaluation by a Record Administrator. To find your designated station, call (214) 877-8423 or send us an email at info@cvrs.com. Alternatively, we offer an online chat feature through our patient portal to send questions to a Vice Management Specialist. The portal can also be used to schedule in-person or virtual appointments. Non-urgent matters can only be addressed by your local CVRS station.

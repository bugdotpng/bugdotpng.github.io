Any updates made to the VRC after April 9, 2025* will be documented and outlined below in reverse-chronological order. CVRS is committed to transparently and honestly communicating the fruits of our dedicated research team. The information presented in this compendium has undergone rigorous testing so as to ensure accuracy. Lead Station Aggregators across the country contribute to the research database, which has been reformatted and presented in a layman-friendly way as the Vice Research Compendium. As new discoveries and research breakthroughs are made and verified, they will be added to the compendium and noted below.

July 19, 2026

v5.1.2

Content Expansion

New information has been provided regarding the --omicron subclass. Information on the types of blights has also been rearranged, adding the designation of IntraBranch and InterBranch blights.

March 5, 2026

v5.1.1

Minor Updates and Content Clarification

Further clarification has been provided for --omicron subclasses and their behavior in regards to rumina.

December 28, 2025

v5.1.0

Content Structure Overhaul

The VRC has received a drastic overhaul in content presentation. In addition to deeper dives on individual classifications that now have their own dedicated pages, the main VRC pages have been split into multiple pages for easier browsing. The anomaly repository has also been made public.

April 9, 2025

v5.0.0

Inaugural Public Release

This is the first version of the Vice Research Compendium that is publicly available and does not require a request to be fulfilled by a Vice Management Administrator. After popular demand, we have elected to publish this information publicly so as to not place it behind a paywall; though, we still recommend seeing one of our dedicated agents to receive proper care if you believe you are infected with a Vice. Many classifications can be tricky, and not all experiences will be covered in this compendium. No amount of published research will ever be able to replace the dedicated care and attention provided by our staff.
